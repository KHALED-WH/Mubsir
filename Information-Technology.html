Brief

Information Technology (IT) is a dynamic and ever-evolving field focused on the use of computers and telecommunications to store, retrieve, transmit, and manipulate data. It prepares students to manage and support computer systems, networks, and software applications.

Key Areas of Study

Networking

Study of computer networks, including their design, implementation, and management.

Cybersecurity

Techniques and practices to protect information systems from cyber threats.

Cloud Computing

Utilization of remote servers hosted on the internet to store, manage, and process data.

Skills Developed

  • Knowledge of networking principles and protocols
  • Expertise in database design and management
  • Understanding of cybersecurity threats and countermeasures

Career Opportunities

Network Administrator

Manages and maintains computer networks, ensuring reliability and security.

Cybersecurity Analyst

Protects information systems from cyber threats by implementing security measures.

Benefits of the Major

  • High Demand in various industries.
  • Versatility to work in diverse tech roles.
