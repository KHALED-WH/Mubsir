Brief

Nuclear Engineering is a specialized field that focuses on the application of nuclear processes and radiation for a variety of purposes, including energy production, medical treatments, and industrial applications.

Key Areas of Study

Nuclear Reactor Design

Principles used to design and analyze nuclear reactors for safe energy production.

Radiation Protection and Safety

Techniques to protect people and environment from harmful effects of radiation.

Skills Developed

  • Strong foundation in advanced mathematics and physics
  • Proficiency in radiation protection and safety protocols

Career Opportunities

Nuclear Engineer

Designs, operates, and maintains nuclear reactors and systems.

Benefits of the Major

  • Contribution to clean, safe, and efficient energy production.
  • Involvement in developing advanced nuclear and medical technologies.
